Research Experience

Ph.D. Researcher — INRS (Canada) / University of Bordeaux (France) Feb 2023 — Present

  • Control of intervalley scattering in $Bi_2Te_3$ via temperature‑dependent band renormalization: We demonstrate that electron-phonon-mediated intervalley scattering plays a pivotal role in determining its electron dynamics and is highly sensitive to subtle variations in the bulk band dispersion.
  • Role of defect states in semiconducting $1T-SnS_2$: We provide fundamental insights into how in-gap states influence the optical properties and ultrafast carrier dynamics of $1T-SnS_2$ based on advanced TR-ARPES measurements.
  • Floquet-Bloch Engineering in 2D Materials: Investigating the transient generation of Floquet-Bloch states in $Bi_2Te_3$ via MIR pump pulses and their momentum warping implications.
  • Charge-density-wave fluctuations in $TiSe_2$: Probing the dynamics of charge density waves to understand their effect on electronic properties.
  • Unconventional marriage between high-temperature superconductivity and pseudogap in cuprates: Investigating hybridization between the main anti-bonding band and its first-order superstructure replica.

M.Tech Thesis — IIT Delhi Aug 2021 — Jun 2022

  • Second Harmonic Generation from Artificial Atoms and Lattices: Investigated nonlinear optical phenomena in artificial lattices using femtosecond lasers. Conducted simulations with Lumerical and COMSOL; developed metamaterial using sputtering and electron beam lithography techniques.

M.Sc Thesis — Presidency University, Kolkata Aug 2018 — Aug 2019

  • Study The Physics of Blazar Using Optical and Gamma ray Outbursts: Analyzed and modeled outburst characteristics and radiation mechanisms in blazars using C, Gnuplot, and Qtiplot.
